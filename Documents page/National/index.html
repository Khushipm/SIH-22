For National Scholarships

Please upload a scanned copy of the following documents in ONE pdf document only: (Size < 10mb)

  1. Copies of mark sheet of standard Xth / XIIth / Others as applicable
  2. Copy of annual family income certificate
  3. Copy of admission letter to Diploma/Degree course.
  4. Copy of Tuition Fee Receipt
  5. Copy of Bank Pass Book
  6. Copy of AADHAR card.
  7. Attested Copy of SC/ST/OBC Certificate. (mandatory in case of candidates claiming benefit of reservation policy).

For International Scholarships

Please upload a scanned copy of the following documents in ONE pdf document only:(Size < 10mb)

  1. Copy of passport/ visa/ ID
  2. Copy of transcripts / diplomas
  3. Statement Of Purpose
  4. CV / Resume
  5. Standardized test scores - GRE / IELTS/ SAT... etc
  6. Medical certificate
  7. Parent's annual income slip
